King of the Hill

Best of 3


Rules

  • 1. Standard legal cards only.
  • 2. Roll a dice at the beginning of the first match. Winner chooses to be on the play or on the draw.
  • 3. The loser of a match in a best of 3 decides weather to be on the play or on the draw.
  • 4. Winner stays as the reigning king, loser makes room for a new challenger.
  • 5. Challenger always decides weather or not he wants to be on the draw or on the play.
  • 6. The reigning king cannot sideboard until he has been dethroned.
